Project Overview

This project focused on analyzing and improving business processes within a simulated organization. Our team conducted a full process analysis cycle - from identifying existing pain points to proposing system-level solutions. We evaluated inventory tracking, customer relationship management, and other operational workflows through SWOT and feasibility analyses, ultimately developing a systems plan for a cloud-based implementation.

Tools & Skills

  • Microsoft Visio - business process modeling (as-is / to-be diagrams).
  • SWOT & feasibility analysis (technical, operational, economic.)
  • Gantt chart development & systems planning documentation.
  • User personas and user stories for requirements definition.

Reflection

Completing this Process Analysis and Systems Planning project deepened my understanding of how information systems support organizational efficiency. Mapping business workflows through As-Is and To-Be models gave me a clearer view of how process improvement aligns with system design. The feasibility study was especially eye-opening - balancing cost, operational impact, and technology choices built a foundation for strategic systems thinking. This project strengthened both my analytical and documentation skills, preparing me to bridge business analysis with systems implementation in real projects.
