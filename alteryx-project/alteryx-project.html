Project Overview

This Business Intelligence project analyzed the Global Superstore dataset to uncover profitability drivers, customer behavior, and operational inefficiencies across regions and product categories. Using Alteryx for data preparation & analytical workflows and Tableau for visualization, our team translated transactional data into actionable business recommendations.

My individual contributions focused on customer segmentation, customer loyalty by region, cross-sell potential, and return behavior analysis. I designed and executed multiple Alteryx workflows to clean, aggregate, and engineer metrics, then built Tableau visualizations to communicate patterns related to retention, revenue opportunities, and margin impact.

Tools & Skills

  • Data preparation, filtering, joins, and aggregation using Alteryx workflows.
  • Metric creation including profit margin, return rate, buyer type (one-time vs repeat), and sales contribution.
  • Interactive data visualization and dashboard development using Tableau.
  • Translating analytical results into clear, business-focused insights.

My Contributions

Customer Segmentation (Tableau)

I analyzed customer segments using sales and profit metrics to understand how different customer groups contributed to overall performance. Tableau dashboards highlighted which segments generated the most revenue and profit, helping identify priority customer groups.

Customer Loyalty by Region (Alteryx & Tableau)

Using Alteryx, I classified customers as one-time or repeat buyers based on order history and summarized loyalty rates by region. Tableau visualizations showed that most regions were dominated by one-time buyers, while a few regions demonstrated stronger repeat behavior - revealing opportunities for region-specific retention strategies.

Cross-Sell Potential (Alteryx & Tableau)

I built Alteryx workflows to identify frequently co-purchased product sub-categories and aggregated combined sales values. These results were visualized in Tableau to highlight strong product pairings that could support bundling or recommendation strategies.

Return Insights (Alteryx & Tableau)

I joined orders and returns data to calculate return rates and analyze their effect on profitability. Tableau dashboards revealed that returns had a disproportionate impact on profit in certain categories, pointing to areas where reducing returns could directly improve margins.
