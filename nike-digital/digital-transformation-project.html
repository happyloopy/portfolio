Project Overview

As part of a team-based consulting project, we developed a hypothetical digital transformation proposal for Nike, focusing on how emerging technologies could reshape its customer experience and operations. Working under the name HypnoTech Consulting LLC, our team analyzed Nike‚Äôs current position in the athletic and athleisure market and designed a multi-year roadmap centered on four pillars: IoT-enabled footwear, 3D and 4D printing for adaptive products, a subscription-based rental model, and a redesigned mobile app featuring AI recommendations and AR try-on. The project combined market research, technology selection, financial projections, and risk analysis to imagine how Nike could maintain its leadership in a rapidly evolving digital landscape, while remaining entirely hypothetical and classroom-based.

Reflection

This hypothetical Nike project helped me practice thinking like a digital transformation consultant rather than just a technology user. Translating broad ideas such as IoT wearables or 4D-printed shoes into a phased roadmap with projected financial outcomes pushed me to connect innovation with feasibility, cost, and risk. Building Tableau dashboards for projected revenues, costs, and operating expenses made the impact of each initiative more concrete, while the mobile app mockups and subscription concepts highlighted how technology can be framed through customer experience. Most importantly, this project reinforced how critical it is to balance ambitious ideas with data privacy, cybersecurity, and change management considerations, especially when working with sensitive customer data and large-scale operational shifts. Although the work is entirely hypothetical and not associated with Nike, it strengthened my confidence in structuring digital strategy proposals for real-world organizations.
